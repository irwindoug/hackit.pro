Overview

Purpose

In an ever growing online connected world, have you ever wondered if your personal information was safe? How would you know if someone stole your identity, was able to see your sensitive information such as your bank account, or had access to your social media accounts? With HackIT Pro, you can learn how to safeguard your information from those with malicious intent. Gain insight on how to remain anonymous while still being able to connect with your loved ones online, and keeping your sensitive information personal. Keep your information from being hacked by learning tips and tricks online at HackIT Pro today!

Audience

Are you a tech professional or do you feel your smart phone smarter than you? Regardless of whether you’ve been around since the first computer or whether you just got your first phone, learning how to take care of your information is as important as driving a car. Just like the road, there are rules on the digital highway to information, but if you don’t know how to maneuver around, you are bound for disaster! Learn from a hackspert as to how to keep your data out of trouble and keep your information safe!

Typography

Heading Font: IBM Plex Serif

I wanted to keep with the format of my logo, which is a Serif font, along with utilizing a font that is more aligned with a programming font style. Since IBM is very closely tied with programming, I decided to go with it!

Paragraph Font: Ubuntu

Also going along with my programming font style theme, but I wanted to differentiate between headers and text. I therefore went with a sans-serif font style for my paragraphs, as it is more eye appealing when reading larger amount of text.
